HOW TO USE THIS INDEX
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  - Order: Do the blocks in sequence; later blocks assume the earlier ones.
  - One by one: Pick the next block (or the next topic inside a block), and we
    can go through it in detail with your actual project (e.g. "explain Block 3"
    or "explain 7.3 with our code").
  - Checkpoints:
    * After Block 6 you understand the single-service REST + validation + errors.
    * After Block 8 you understand the full ticket-triage flow.
    * After Block 9 you understand the microservices split.

  To start: say which block you want to learn first (e.g. "Block 1" or "Block 2
  topic 2.3") and we'll go step by step.
